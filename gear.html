Electronics

  • M1 14" MacBook Pro - The best thing I can say is that I never notice anything about this computer.
  • Apple Studio Display - Crystal clear picture for connecting to the MBP but I would consider buying something bigger that works with other devices if I could do it over.
  • 11" iPad Pro with Cellular - The perfect daily computer except for the restrictions of iPadOS.
  • iPhone 15 Pro Max - iPhones are pretty boring at this point and I also find this size to be annoying so looking forward to either downsizing or getting the rumored fold phone.
  • Rolling Square inCharge XL 6-in-1 Multi Charging Cable - I've slowly been purging all of my non USB-C devices but it still comes up somehow.
  • Baseus USB C to USB C Cable, 100W Retractable - Why tie cables when you can retract them!

EDC

  • Evergoods CTB 20 or Aer City Pack Pro - After having purchased almost every backpack in the last five years, I've decided that the CTB20 and the Aer CPP are the two closest to perfect. I prefer the vertical nature of the CPP but I do like the incremental pockets of the CTB20.
  • Aer Day Sling 3 Max X-Pac - Perfect size for a vacation or a long day out in the city. My only quibble is the buckle can be a little fiddly.
  • Hero Clip Carabiners - Useful for hanging stuff including my kiddo's jacket and water bottle.
  • Owala Water Bottle - Having tried every major water bottle, the Owala one is the easiest to clean and best cap/lid system.
  • WUBEN G5 Rechargeable EDC Flashlight - Very light and very bright! The light can also pivot so you can clip it on your clothes and use it as a makeshift headlight.
  • Nitecore NB Air 5000mAh Ultra Lightweight - For peace of mind to have an extra little juice if any device dies out.
  • Pilot Juice Up 04 Retractable Gel Ink Pen (0.4mm) - The best everyday cheap pen that writes like a charm.
